Welcome to my humble portfolio page. This is a page of collections, consisting of all my projects I have accumulated since the start of my developer career.


While studying for Space Science as an undergrad at York University, I discovered I was more interested in programming, but I wanted to complete my degree because I was very deeply invested. It was still a very interesting subject, but programming was gradually becoming more desired as a daily activity for me. I believed I could leverage my knowledge in math and physics and combine it with computer science to solve problems in software engineering. With a history of programming, ample self learning and a boost from a bootcamp, I am confident in delivering efficient code as a Software Developer.
